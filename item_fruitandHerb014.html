How To Taste Tea?

  • Appearance

    Colour

    We are looking for a bright, jewel-like colour.

    Physical Appearance

    we are looking for shiny, slightly oily and bright

    Clarity

    We're looking for a great colour. And to see that the leaves have become plump and whole again.

  • Aroma

    Dog Action

    a quick, shallow inhalation through your nose

    Deep Inhalations

    Hold the coffee close to nose and take a deep breath

  • Flavour

    Head notes

    the ones that give you the initial impression

    body notes

    These give you the overall lasting impression and character

    tail notes

    the ones that linger and stay with you after the liquid has passed from your mouth

  • Mouth Feel

    Mouth feel is all about the sensations you feel in your mouth when you taste tea. And of course, different teas trigger different sensations.
